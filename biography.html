30.12.1956. Born in Niš.

1975. Finished secondary school of architecture in Belgrade.

1983. Graduated at school of architecture at the University of Belgrade.

1987. Passed the official professional exam. Participated in Yugoslavian and international architectural competitions and in which he won several awards. Works exhibited in Gallery of Architecture 1987, 1988. and published in journals for architecture.

1986. Won a second prize for the "Tri lista duvana" business center competition and a third prize for the "Beobanka" office building competition.

1987. Was awarded a special prize for exhibited works in the Gallery of architecture along with architects S.Martinović, N.Minjević and B. Slavica.

1988. Won a second prize for the internation competition in Bagdad,Iraq for the project presidential residence. Also was working on a project under the auspices of the Serbian Academy of Sciences and Art called "Belgrade on the threshold of the III millennium".

1990. Worked and finished the shopping mall project in Obrenovac. Also won a second prize in a Belgrade competition for the Palace "Genex chrystal" in Knez Mihajlova street.

1991. Won a first prize for the Tennis business cultural center competition organized by "Mecon" in Novi Beograd.

1992. Worked on an exclusive club-hotel project "Weron Elen stone" at Ohrid lake in Macedonia.

1993. Worked on a "Berni" salon with N. Minjević and also on a residential-business block "Blok-Nevesinjska-Kkursulina" with J. Lalić, H. Dimitrijević and N. Minjević in Belgrade.

1994. Worked on a club "Trezor" with N. Minjević in Belgrade.

1995. Worked on a project cafe "Doróol" in Belgrade and a fitness club in Moscow along with N.Minjević.

1996. Won a second prize for the open market and shopping mall "Liman" competition in Novi Sad.

1997. Worked on a rehabilitation center "Uhta" in Russia and on a business center "CORA" in Moscow.

1998. Worked on a presidential residence "Jeljcin" in Moscow.

1999. Worked on a family house project for Z.Djaković.

2003. Worked on a branch office "Privredna Banka Beograd" in Zemun, Novi Beograd and Banovo Brdo.

2004. Worked on a Sports center "Baza Odiha" in Moscow and the project "Cukaricka padina" residential building was finished.

2006. Won a first prize in a competition for a business-residential building situated right next to the famous Sveti Sava temple.

2006-2015. Worked in SAGA d.o.o. as an architect.

2016. Won a GRAND PRIX (first prize) in a Salon of Architecture in Belgrade, Serbia.

2017. Works on a enterior design for the hospital "SVETI VID" in Belgrade.
